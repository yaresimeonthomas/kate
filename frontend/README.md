# Kate AOS - Authentication Guide

You do not need a pre-configured username or password to log in! Because this application is connected directly to your live Firebase project, you will create your own real account.

## How to access the app:

1. Open the application in your browser. You will see the Sign In screen.
2. Click the **"Don't have an account? Sign up"** link at the bottom of the card.
3. The form will change to the "Create Account" view.
4. Enter your **Business Name**, a valid **Email**, and a **Password**.
5. Click **"Create Account"**.

### What happens next?
* A real user account is instantly created in your Firebase Authentication dashboard.
* A user document is created in your Firestore `users` collection with the `starter` tier.
* You will be automatically redirected to the **Onboarding Flow** to chat with the Onboarding Agent and set up your business context. 

Once onboarding is complete, you can log out and log back in anytime using the email and password you just created!
